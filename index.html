Welcome to Astha Shakti

Astha Shakti empowers underprivileged differently able children through handmade craft sales, like candles, fostering education skill development and brighter futures.Through its multifaceted initiatives, from supporting underprivileged children with education and nutrition to celebrating cultural festivals and national occasions in schools, Astha Shakti embodies the spirit of compassion, empowerment, and inclusivity.

About Us

Astha Shakti is a registered charitable organization working for the welfare of underprivileged and downtrodden children who are struggling to get even a square meal. Our vision concept is "Food is the Gateway to Good Health and Education", we are running the NUTRI-ED Program under which we are providing Free Nutritive Food with Education.

The Core Objective of Astha Shakti is to serve the Underprivileged, the Economically Deprived through NUTRI-ED Program there by providing opportunity for Education, Skill Development, Economic Rehabilitation and Social Integration.
