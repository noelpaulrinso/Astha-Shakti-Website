Nutrition Program

Astha Shakti's flagship initiative, Nutri-Ed (Nutritive Food with Education), endeavors to offer hygienic sustenance and simplified learning opportunities to underprivileged communities. Recognized by governmental bodies and corporate entities alike for its outstanding societal contributions, Nutri-Ed has undergone significant growth since its establishment in 2009.

Why Nutrition Matters

Malnutrition remains a major barrier to education and development among underprivileged children. At Astha Shakti, we believe that access to nutritious meals is essential for both physical and mental well-being, enabling children to focus on their studies and achieve their full potential.

Our Approach

  • Nutritious Meals: Daily healthy meals that meet the dietary needs of growing children.
  • Hygiene Awareness: Workshops to teach the importance of cleanliness and food safety.
  • Holistic Development: A combination of food, education, and emotional support for overall growth.
  • Community Engagement: Partnering with local organizations and individuals to distribute meals effectively.

Our Impact

Since 2009, the Nutri-Ed program has touched the lives of thousands of children and their families. By ensuring access to balanced meals and quality education, we are not only tackling hunger but also helping to break the cycle of poverty in underprivileged communities.
